A vibecoding project inspired by this FT article and Deutsche Bank Chart
https://www.ft.com/content/16c122a3-d2f3-4ae5-8c98-af1770d99e0f?syn-25a6b1a6=1

Suspecting that sustained weakness across broad macroeconomic indicators pushes the US government and the Fed to enact a response to support the equity market (S&P500), I create a simple market pressure index capturing different aspects of macro conditions with data from FRED. After a threshold is breached for consecutive days, I measure the cumulative price performance for 20 business days (roughly a calendar month).

Looking at the results below do seem to suggest 'buying the dip' in these cases may be profitable.
Interestingly, the signal has triggered on 17 March 2026, as I write this on 25 March 2026. Who knows?
